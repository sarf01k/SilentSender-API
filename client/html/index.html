Say what’s on your mind — anonymously.
Whether it’s praise, criticism, feedback or just something you’ve been meaning to say — SilentSender gives you a safe, anonymous way to send it.

What can you do on SilentSender?

Send Messages

Encourage someone quietly. Call out bad behavior without fear. Say thank you, sorry, or what you really feel  — all without your name attached.

Receive Feedback

Want to know how others see you? Share your link and receive honest messages. The good, the bad, and the brutally honest  — all in one place.

React, Reflect, Reply

You can choose to respond publicly or just sit with the message privately. Either way, it’s yours to deal with, your way.

Why SilentSender?

  • It’s anonymous — no accounts needed to send.
  • It’s for everyone — students, colleagues, teams, friends.
  • It’s open — no filters, no sugarcoating.

* Not every message will be nice. That’s okay.

SilentSender is about honesty. Sometimes that’s uncomfortable. Sometimes it’s beautiful. But it’s always yours.
